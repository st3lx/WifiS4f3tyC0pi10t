Wifi Safety Copilot

Are you afraid that hackers will see everything you do online, infect your devices, or even lock you out? Insecure Wi-Fi gives an attacker full control of your network, allowing them to do these.

Is your home Wi-Fi secure? This is an educational tool that will assist you to find out and fix it together.

This simple tool will guide you through checking your router for common security problems and provide clear, actionable solutions. No technical skills needed!



--- or ---

For more technical users. You'll run a simple script on your computer to automatically gather information.



Automatic Detection

Follow these steps:

  1. Copy the command below for your operating system.
  2. Paste it into your Terminal (Mac/Linux) or Command Prompt (Windows) and press Enter.
  3. Copy the entire text output that the command generates.
  4. Paste it into the text area below and click "Analyze Results".

For Windows (Powershell):

irm https://raw.githubusercontent.com/st3lx/WifiS4f3tyC0pi10t/main/scripts/scan.ps1 | iex

For Mac/Linux (Terminal):

curl -s https://raw.githubusercontent.com/st3lx/WifiS4f3tyC0pi10t/main/scripts/scan.sh | bash


Disclaimer: Use this tool only on networks you own or have permission to test.
This checkup looks for common configuration weaknesses that make hacking easy. It does not monitor live network traffic and cannot tell you if a hacker is actively attacking you right now. For real-time monitoring, consider professional Intrusion Detection Systems (IDS).
